Hungry Geese hungry_geese

hungry_geese is a multi-player version of the snake game in which your agent, a goose:

  • always moves through a rectangular grid, of size rows $\times$ columns, searching for the min_food food randomly situated and avoiding obstacles (itself and other geese).
  • on finding food its length grows by one.
  • on every hunger_rate steps it length reduces by one.
  • on collision with other geese of itself it dies. Note: that this rule is implemented so that a goose is not allowed to reverse direction even when it is of length 1. IMHO this is weird but rather than changing it we will use this to build our first agent.
